Prediction of PM 2.5 in Beijing

Group Project

During a course I followed we had an important project where we had to predict the pm 2.5 by using diﬀerent environmental measures such the concentration of others polluant, the temperature or the precipitation. We used different statistical tools such as correlation or recursive features elimination to identify wich features could be usefull to predict pm 2.5. Then we used K-NN and a Multi-layer perceptrons to make the predictions and we compared the accuracy of those models. Contrary to the prediction of the NOX for the IBGE we only used statistical knowledge for this project.

Key words: Data science, Environmental modeling, Python

Internship at Luxembourgishe redcross in Niamey(Niger)

Internship

I made an intership during my third year of bachelor in a tripartite partnership between the university of Gembloux, the university Abdou-Moumouni from Niamey and the Luxembourgish Red-Cross.
