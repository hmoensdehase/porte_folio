Prediction of PM 2.5 in Beijing

Group Project

During a course I followed we had an important project where we had to predict the pm 2.5 by using diﬀerent environmental measures such the concentration of others polluant, the temperature or the precipitation. We used different statistical tools such as correlation of recursive features elimination to identify wich features could be usefull to predict the pm 2.5. Then we used K-NN and a Multi-layer perceptrons to make the predictions and we compared the accuracy of those models. Contrary to the prediction of the NOX for the IBGE we only used statistical knowledge for this project.
